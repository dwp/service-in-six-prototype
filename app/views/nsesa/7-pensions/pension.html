Pensions Are you getting regular payments from a pension or annuity?

You do not need to tell us about single lump sum pension payments.

Pensions include:

  • workplace pensions
  • personal pensions
  • public service pensions
  • stakeholder pensions
  • a pension from a self employed pension scheme

An annuity is an insurance product that allows you to swap your pension savings for a guaranteed regular income that will last for the rest of your life.
