Time spent abroad Have you lived abroad with your partner who was posted there with the armed forces?

You may have been awarded National Insurance credits through the Ministry of Defence if your partner was posted overseas by the armed forces. These can help you qualify for New Style Jobseeker's Allowance.

You must have:

  • gone with them on their overseas posting
  • been their civil partner or their spouse at the time
  • lived with them for at least a week
